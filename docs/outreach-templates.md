## 1. Cold outreach: Brand-direct wholesale account

**When to use:** Brand sells directly to retailers (no distributor needed). Most CPG
brands have a wholesale form on their site. If they don't, this email goes to whoever
handles wholesale inquiries (often `wholesale@brand.com`, `sales@brand.com`, or via
LinkedIn to a Sales / Trade Marketing manager).

**From:** `ricardo@pernikuswholesale.com`
**Subject:** Wholesale account inquiry — Pernikus LLC (Florida)

```
Hi [First Name or "[Brand] Wholesale Team"],

I'm Ricardo Siberio, owner of Pernikus LLC, a Florida-based multi-channel
retailer of consumer packaged goods. We operate an Amazon FBA storefront,
direct-to-consumer e-commerce at pernikuswholesale.com, and supply a small
network of regional B2B accounts.

We're interested in opening an authorized wholesale account with [Brand].
Specifically, we'd like to carry [2–3 SKUs by name] for resale through
our Amazon and direct channels under your authorized-seller program, with
full MAP compliance.

For your review:
  • EIN-verified Florida LLC, established 2023
  • Active FL Annual Resale Certificate (DR-13)
  • D-U-N-S Number: 041-702-526
  • W-9 and resale certificate available the same business day
  • Public credentials page at pernikuswholesale.com/credentials
  • [Add when issued: GL + Product Liability insurance]

Could you point me to the right person on your wholesale team, or share
your reseller application packet? I'm happy to start with a small initial
order to demonstrate sell-through.

Thanks,
Ricardo Siberio
Pernikus LLC
ricardo@pernikuswholesale.com  •  (407) 881-7996
pernikuswholesale.com
```

**When to use:** Brand has a formal Amazon authorized-reseller program (common in
beauty, supplements, premium snacks). Often a form, but if you have to email,
this is the version.

**From:** `ricardo@pernikuswholesale.com`
**Subject:** Authorized reseller application — Pernikus LLC

```
Hi [First Name or "[Brand] Channel Team"],

I'd like to apply for authorized-reseller status with [Brand] for the Amazon
US marketplace.

About Pernikus LLC:
  • Florida LLC, established 2023, EIN-verified
  • Active Amazon FBA storefront in good standing (no policy issues, no IP claims)
  • Multi-channel sell-through: Amazon + direct e-commerce at pernikuswholesale.com
  • Strict MAP compliance and channel discipline
  • Authorized-source sourcing only — no diversion, no gray-market

We protect the brands we represent. We invest in listing quality
(professional product photography, optimized titles and bullets, A+ content
where supported), and we coordinate proactively with brand teams on any
listing or pricing concerns.

process? I'd like to start with [1–3 specific SKUs] and grow the account
based on sell-through.

Documentation packet (W-9, FL Resale Certificate, D-U-N-S 041-702-526)
available same-day from compliance@pernikuswholesale.com. Certificate of
Insurance is in placement and will be shared as soon as bound.

**When to use:** Most portals ask for a free-text "About your business"
or "Why do you want to carry our brands" field. Use this:

```
Pernikus LLC is a Florida-based multi-channel retailer of consumer packaged
goods, established in 2023 and operating from Orlando, FL. We sell to U.S.
consumers through Amazon FBA, our direct e-commerce site at
pernikuswholesale.com, and a small network of regional B2B accounts.

Our model is intentionally focused on consumable, replenishment-driven CPG
categories — grocery, beverages, household essentials, and health & beauty.
We respect MAP policies, brand authorization restrictions, and channel
guidelines. We do not engage in unauthorized cross-border diversion or
gray-market sourcing.

We carry vendor-onboarding documentation: EIN-verified, active FL Annual
Resale Certificate, signed W-9 ready to issue, and D-U-N-S Number
041-702-526. Certificate of Insurance is in placement. Current standing
on each item is published at pernikuswholesale.com/credentials and shared
directly by our compliance team at compliance@pernikuswholesale.com.

## General tips

1. **Always reference the brand by name** in cold outreach. Generic emails get
   ignored. "Pet brand wholesale inquiry" → trash. "Authorized reseller
   application — [Specific Brand]" → opened.

2. **Show that you understand their channel concerns.** Brands worry about MAP,
   diversion, and unauthorized listings. Address these upfront.

3. **Lead with documentation readiness.** The biggest objection from a
   distributor compliance team is "this seller will be hard to onboard."
   Removing that fear is half the battle.

4. **Keep it under 200 words on first contact.** Long emails read as desperate.

5. **Always include phone number and physical address in your signature.** This
   is the #1 thing distinguishing legitimate B2B from arbitrage operators.

6. **Send Tuesday–Thursday, 9–11 AM in the recipient's time zone.** Higher open
   rates than Monday or Friday.

7. **CC `compliance@` on document-heavy threads.** Signals organizational depth.

8. **Don't apologize for being small.** "We are a small but growing operation"
   is a credibility-killer. Just describe what you do without qualifiers.

9. **Track every application.** Use a Google Sheet with columns: Brand,
   Contact, Date Sent, Status, Next Action, Notes. This is your sales pipeline.

10. **Expect a 10-20% positive response rate on cold outreach** to good-fit brands.
    Higher if you have personal connections, lower for top-tier names.
